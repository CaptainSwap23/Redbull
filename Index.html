Drink Red Bull

Red Bull is a brand of energy drinks sold by Austrian company Red Bull GmbH. With 38%, Red Bull has the highest market share among energy drinks as of 2019. Since its launch in 1987, more than 82 billion cans of Red Bull have been sold worldwide, including 7.9 billion in 2020.
